<p><p>Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) Software comes in various types to manage different aspects of the product development process. Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) software is used for simulation and analysis. Finite Element Analysis (FEA) software helps in analyzing product structures. Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM) software focuses on optimizing manufacturing processes. Other types of PLM software include product configuration management, quality management, and compliance management solutions.</p></p>
