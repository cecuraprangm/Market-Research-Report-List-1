<p><p>Pressed ceramic packages are utilized in various markets such as ceramic-metal sealing (CERTM), glass-metal sealing (GTMS), passivation glass, transponder glass, and reed glass. CERTM packages offer superior hermetic sealing for electronic components, while GTMS packages provide a reliable seal between glass and metal components. Passivation glass packages are used for protecting sensitive electronic devices, while transponder glass packages are commonly found in RFID technology. Reed glass packages are used in applications requiring fast response times and high reliability.</p></p>
