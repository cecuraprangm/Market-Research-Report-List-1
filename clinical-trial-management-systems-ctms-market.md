<p><p>Clinical Trial Management Systems (CTMS) are software systems used in the management and tracking of clinical trials. There are three main types of CTMS markets: Cloud-based (SaaS) CTMS, Licensed Enterprise (On-premise) CTMS, and Web-based (Hosted) CTMS. Cloud-based CTMS are accessed via the internet, allowing for easy scalability and lower upfront costs. Licensed Enterprise CTMS are installed on-site and offer more customization and control. Web-based CTMS are hosted on a remote server, offering flexibility and accessibility without the need for on-site installation.</p></p>
