<p><p>Affiliate Channel Partner Programs are market types where businesses pay their partners for driving sales, leads, or clicks to their products or services. In Cost-Per-Sale (CPS), partners receive a commission based on the number of successful sales they generate. Cost-Per-Lead (CPL) pays partners for each qualified lead they provide. Cost-Per-Click (CPC) compensates partners for each click generated on their referral links. These program types help businesses increase their reach and drive more revenue by leveraging the efforts of their affiliate partners.</p></p>
