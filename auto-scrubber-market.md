<p><p>The auto-scrubber market is divided into three main types: push-type scrubbers, ride-on scrubbers, and rear-standing scrubbers. Push-type scrubbers are manually pushed and are suitable for smaller areas. Ride-on scrubbers are larger and are driven by an operator, making them ideal for larger spaces. Rear-standing scrubbers are compact and maneuverable, allowing the operator to stand on the platform while operating the machine. Each type caters to a different set of needs and preferences in the commercial cleaning industry.</p></p>
