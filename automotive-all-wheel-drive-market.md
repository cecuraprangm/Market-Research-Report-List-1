<p><strong>The Automotive All-wheel Drive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-demand All-wheel Drive</li><li>Full-time All-wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive All-wheel Drive (AWD) systems can be categorized into two main types - On-demand AWD and Full-time AWD. On-demand AWD systems allow vehicles to operate in front-wheel drive mode most of the time, but can automatically redirect power to the rear wheels when additional traction is needed. Full-time AWD systems continuously distribute power to all four wheels, providing constant traction in various driving conditions. Both types of AWD systems offer improved stability and performance, catering to different driver preferences and needs.</p></p>
