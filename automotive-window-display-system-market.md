<p><p>The automotive window display system market consists of two main types: windshield display and combiner display. Windshield display systems project information directly onto the windshield, allowing drivers to access important information without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner display systems, on the other hand, overlay information onto a separate display near the windshield. Both types of display systems provide drivers with essential information such as speed, navigation directions, and warning alerts to enhance safety and convenience while driving.</p></p>
