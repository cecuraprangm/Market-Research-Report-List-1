<p><p>Power window regulators are mechanisms that control the movement of car windows. There are three main types: Rope Wheel Type, Fork Arm Type, and Flexible Shaft Type. Rope Wheel Type regulators use a pulley system to raise and lower the window. Fork Arm Type regulators have fork-shaped arms that push the window up and down. Flexible Shaft Type regulators use a flexible shaft connected to a motor to control the window's movement. Each type has its own unique design and mechanism for efficient window operation in vehicles.</p></p>
