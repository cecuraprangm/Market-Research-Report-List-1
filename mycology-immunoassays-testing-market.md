<p><strong>The Mycology Immunoassays Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA</li><li>Rapid Tests</li><li>ELISPOT</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mycology Immunoassays Testing Market involves the evaluation of fungal infections using various methods. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is a common technique that detects and quantifies fungal antigens or antibodies. Rapid tests are fast, point-of-care tests that provide quick results. ELISPOT (Enzyme-Linked Immunospot Assay) identifies and measures the number of immune cells producing a specific response against fungal antigens. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies and detects fungal DNA, providing sensitive and specific diagnoses. These methods play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing fungal infections.</p></p>
