<p><p>The Influenza Diagnostics market includes several types of diagnostic methods. Rapid Influenza Diagnostic Tests (RIDT) provide quick results by detecting viral antigens in the respiratory samples. Reverse Transcriptase Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR) method detects viral RNA, providing high sensitivity and specificity. Cell culture method involves virus isolation and replication in cells, allowing further analysis. Other diagnostic methods like immunofluorescence assays and serology tests offer alternative approaches. Each type of diagnostic method has its advantages and limitations, contributing to the overall influenza diagnostics market.</p></p>
