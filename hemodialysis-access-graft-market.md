<p><p>Hemodialysis access grafts are used to create a connection between an artery and a vein in patients undergoing hemodialysis treatment. There are three types of grafts available in the market: biological grafts, synthetic grafts, and biosynthetic grafts. Biological grafts are made from human or animal tissues, synthetic grafts are man-made using materials like polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), and biosynthetic grafts are a combination of biological and synthetic materials. Each type has its advantages and limitations, and their use depends on the patient's specific needs and medical condition.</p></p>
