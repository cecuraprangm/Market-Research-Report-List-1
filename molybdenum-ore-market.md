<p><strong>The Molybdenum Ore Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Molybdenite (MoS2)</li><li>Associated Ore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molybdenum ore market can be categorized into two main types: primary molybdenite (MoS2) and associated ore. Primary molybdenite refers to pure molybdenum disulfide, which is the most sought-after form of molybdenum ore due to its high molybdenum content. Associated ore, on the other hand, refers to molybdenum found alongside other minerals such as copper or tungsten. While primary molybdenite commands a higher price in the market due to its purity, associated ore is also valued for its molybdenum content and can be processed to extract the valuable metal.</p></p>
