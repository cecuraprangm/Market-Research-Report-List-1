<p><ul><li>Thin Film Magnetoresistive Sensor</li><li>Anisotropic Magnetoresistive Sensor</li><li>Giant Magnetoresistive Sensors</li><li>Tunnel Magnetoresistive Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetoresistive (MR) sensors can be classified into several types based on their structure and operation. Thin film magnetoresistive sensors utilize a thin film of magnetic material to detect changes in magnetic fields. Anisotropic magnetoresistive sensors have a specific directional sensitivity to magnetic fields. Giant magnetoresistive sensors exhibit a larger change in resistance in response to magnetic fields. Tunnel magnetoresistive sensors utilize quantum tunneling to detect magnetic fields. Other types of MR sensors include spin valve sensors and magnetic tunnel junction sensors.</p></p>
