<p><p>Reflux testing and monitoring devices are used to diagnose and manage gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD). The market includes reflux capsule, reflux probe, and reflux catheter. Reflux capsule is a wireless device that measures pH levels in the esophagus. Reflux probe is inserted through the nose to measure acid levels in the esophagus. Reflux catheter is a thin tube that is placed into the esophagus to measure acid levels. These devices help doctors determine the severity of GERD and develop treatment plans accordingly.</p></p>
