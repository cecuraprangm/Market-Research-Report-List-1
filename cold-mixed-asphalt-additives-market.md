<p><p>Cold mixed asphalt additives market types include Polymeric Modifiers, which enhance the performance of asphalt by improving its elasticity and durability. Anti-strip & Adhesion Promoters help improve the adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, reducing moisture damage. Emulsifiers are used to create stable asphalt emulsions for cold mix applications. Chemical Modifiers modify the properties of asphalt to meet specific performance requirements. Other additives include fibers, rejuvenators, and anti-oxidants that offer additional benefits to cold mixed asphalt mixtures.</p></p>
