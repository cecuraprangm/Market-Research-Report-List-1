<p><strong>The Non-Contact Radar Level Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Guided-Wave Radar Level Transmitter</li><li>Non-Contact Wave Radar Level Transmitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact radar level transmitters are used in various industries for accurate level measurement of liquids and solids. Guided-wave radar level transmitters use a probe that guides the radar signal to the material being measured, providing precise measurements in applications with changing process conditions. Non-contact radar level transmitters, on the other hand, emit radar waves directly into the material without the need for physical contact, making them suitable for dusty, corrosive, or high-temperature environments. Both types offer reliable and accurate level measurement solutions for different industrial processes.</p></p>
