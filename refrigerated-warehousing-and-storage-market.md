<p><p>Refrigerated warehousing and storage market types refer to different methods used for preserving and storing goods at low temperatures. Vapor compression is a widely used technology that involves compressing and cooling refrigerants to remove heat from the storage area. Evaporative cooling utilizes the evaporation of water to decrease the temperature inside the storage facility. Blast freezing rapidly freezes products by using high-velocity cold air. Other market types may include technologies such as cryogenic freezing or ice storage, which use extremely low temperatures or ice blocks to maintain cool conditions for storage.</p></p>
