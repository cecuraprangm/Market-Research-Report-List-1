<p><p>Micro prismatic reflective sheeting is available in three main types based on the design of the reflective prisms: Long-distance Type, Short-distance Type, and Full-prism Type. Long-distance Type sheeting is designed to reflect light over longer distances, making it suitable for road signs and traffic signals. Short-distance Type sheeting is ideal for shorter distance visibility, often used in license plates and reflective clothing. Full-prism Type sheeting contains dense and full prisms for maximum reflectivity, commonly used in high-visibility safety apparel and equipment.</p></p>
