<p><ul><li>Cationic Polymer Type</li><li>Resin Type</li><li>Cross-linking Reactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber fixing agents are used to improve the performance and quality of textiles by enhancing their strength and durability. The market is segmented into three main types: Cationic Polymer Type, Resin Type, and Cross-linking Reactive Type. Cationic Polymer Type agents are positively charged and adhere well to negatively charged fibers. Resin Type agents provide a strong bond between fibers, while Cross-linking Reactive Type agents create covalent bonds between molecules, improving the overall stability of the fabric. Each type offers unique benefits for various textile applications.</p></p>
