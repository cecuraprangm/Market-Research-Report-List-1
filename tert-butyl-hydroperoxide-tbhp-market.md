<p><p>Tert-butyl hydroperoxide (TBHP) is primarily produced through four different market types: TBA Hydroperoxide Method, Isobutene Hydroperoxide Method, Isobutane Oxidation Method, and Gesso Reagent Synthesis Method. TBA Hydroperoxide Method involves the oxidation of Tertiary Butyl Alcohol (TBA) using hydrogen peroxide. Isobutene Hydroperoxide Method involves the reaction of isobutene with hydrogen peroxide. Isobutane Oxidation Method involves the oxidation of isobutane gas with oxygen. Gesso Reagent Synthesis Method involves the reaction of tert-butyl alcohol with hydrogen peroxide. Each method results in the production of TBHP for various industrial applications.</p></p>
