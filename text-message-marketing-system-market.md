<p><p>Text message marketing systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. </p><p>Cloud-based systems operate on a remote server accessible through the internet. These systems are cost-effective as they eliminate the need for enterprises to invest in infrastructure and maintenance.</p><p>On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed and operated on the company's own servers and infrastructure.
