<p><p>Vegan milk, also known as plant-based milk, is a non-dairy alternative to traditional animal-based milk. It is derived from various sources such as soybeans, almonds, coconuts, oats, hemp, rice, and peas. Vegan milk offers a cruelty-free, ethical option for consumers who avoid or prefer alternatives to dairy products due to dietary, environmental, or animal welfare concerns.</p><p>The global vegan milk market has witnessed substantial growth in recent years and is projected to continue growing at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing awareness about the health benefits of plant-based diets has led to a rise in demand for vegan milk. It is often lower in calories and saturated fat compared to traditional dairy milk, making it an attractive option for health-conscious consumers.</p><p>Additionally, the growing lactose intolerance and dairy allergies among individuals have fueled the demand for vegan milk. Many people find plant-based alternatives easier to digest and less likely to cause allergic reactions. Moreover, the expanding vegan population and the adoption of veganism as a lifestyle choice have further propelled the market growth of vegan milk.</p><p>The latest trend in the vegan milk market is the introduction of innovative flavors and formulations. Manufacturers are constantly launching new varieties of vegan milk to cater to different tastes and preferences of consumers. This includes flavored options such as vanilla, chocolate, and matcha as well as blends with added functional ingredients like vitamins, minerals, and probiotics.</p><p>Furthermore, the availability of vegan milk in various formats, including shelf-stable cartons, refrigerated bottles, and powdered forms, has contributed to the market's growth. The rising popularity of online retail channels and the expansion of vegan milk product offerings in supermarkets and specialty stores have also boosted market sales.</p><p>In conclusion, the vegan milk market is experiencing significant growth due to factors such as increased health awareness, rising lactose intolerance, growing vegan population, and the launch of innovative flavors. With a projected CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period, the market is expected to witness further expansion in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>Vegan milk market offers a variety of alternatives to traditional dairy milk. Almond milk, made from ground almonds and water, is a popular option known for its mild taste. Coconut milk, made from the liquid inside coconuts, has a creamy texture and adds a tropical flavor. Rice milk, made from milled rice and water, is the least allergenic option.
