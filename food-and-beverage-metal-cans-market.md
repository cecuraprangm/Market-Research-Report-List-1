<p><p>The food and beverage metal cans market consists of two main types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by assembling three separate components - the body, top, and bottom - using a welding or soldering process. These cans are highly versatile and can be easily customized in terms of size and shape. On the other hand, two-piece cans are manufactured from a single piece of metal, which is drawn and formed into the can shape. These cans are commonly used for beverages and offer cost-effective packaging solutions.</p></p>
