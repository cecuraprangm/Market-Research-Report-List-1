<p><p>Contrast media injectors are devices used to administer contrast agents during various medical imaging procedures. In the vascular market, there are two main types of contrast media injectors - single-head and dual-head. </p><p>Single-head injectors have one syringe that delivers the contrast agent, while dual-head injectors have two syringes for simultaneous injection of multiple agents or to increase injection speed. Single-head injectors are typically used for basic imaging procedures, while dual-head injectors are preferred for complex vascular imaging and interventions that require multiple contrast agents or rapid injection rates.</p></p>
