<p><p>Sports tourism market types can be classified into five categories based on the level of motivation behind the travel. Very motivated refers to individuals who specifically plan their trips to attend sporting events. Partially motivated tourists engage in sports-related activities while also pursuing other non-sporting interests. Accessory tourists participate in sports events due to their affiliation or companionship with athletes or participants. Accidental tourists happen to come across sports events during their leisure or business trips. Lastly, not motivated tourists have no particular interest in sports but may engage in sports-related activities as a secondary option while traveling.</p></p>
