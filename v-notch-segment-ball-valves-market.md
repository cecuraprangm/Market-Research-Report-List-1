<p><p>The V-Notch Segment Ball Valves market can be divided into two types based on their design: Flanged and Flangeless. Flanged valves are equipped with protruding rims around the valve ends for easy attachment to pipelines using bolted connections. On the other hand, Flangeless valves do not have this protruding rim, making them more compact and lightweight. Both types offer efficient flow control and are used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment.</p></p>
