<p><p>Semi-Automatic Stretch Wrappers come in three main types: Rotary Stretch Wrapper, Turntable Stretch Wrapper, and Robotic Stretch Wrapper. </p><p>The Rotary Stretch Wrapper utilizes a rotating arm to apply stretch film around a pallet, while the Turntable Stretch Wrapper rotates the pallet itself to wrap it. Robotic Stretch Wrapper, on the other hand, uses advanced robotic technology to automatically wrap pallets without the need for human intervention.</p><p>Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing options for businesses looking to streamline their wrapping processes.</p></p>
