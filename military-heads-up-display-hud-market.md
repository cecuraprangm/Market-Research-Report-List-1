<p><p>The Military Heads up Display (HUD) market includes two main types: Conventional HUD and AR-Based HUD. Conventional HUD technology projects essential flight information directly onto a transparent screen in a pilot's line of sight. On the other hand, AR-Based HUD technology enhances this information by overlaying virtual elements onto the real-world environment, providing additional situational awareness for the user. Both technologies are crucial in providing crucial information to military personnel during missions.</p></p>
