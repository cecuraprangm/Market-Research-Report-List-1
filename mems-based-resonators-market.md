<p><p>MEMS-based resonators have two main types based on their operation: Series Resonance Type and Parallel Resonance Type. Series Resonance Type resonators have high input impedance and are used in narrowband applications. On the other hand, Parallel Resonance Type resonators have low input impedance and are used in wideband applications. Both types of resonators are crucial in various industries such as telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive for applications like frequency control, timing circuits, and signal processing.</p></p>
