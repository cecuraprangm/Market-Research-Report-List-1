<p><p>Split snowboards come in different market types based on their camber profile. Normal Camber Splitboards have an upward arch between the bindings, providing stability and good edge hold. Reverse Camber Splitboards have a downward arch, making them easier to maneuver and float in powder. Flat Camber Splitboards have a flat base for a more balanced ride. Hybrid Types combine different camber styles for versatility in various terrain. Each type caters to different riding styles and preferences, offering options for all snowboarders.</p></p>
