<p><p>Integrated LED Downlights come in two main market types: Surface Mount and Flush Mount. Surface Mount downlights are installed directly onto the surface of the ceiling, making them easier to install and suitable for spaces with limited ceiling depth. Flush Mount downlights are recessed into the ceiling, providing a seamless and sleek look. They are ideal for spaces where a more streamlined aesthetic is desired. Both types of downlights offer energy-efficient lighting solutions and long-lasting performance, making them popular choices for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
