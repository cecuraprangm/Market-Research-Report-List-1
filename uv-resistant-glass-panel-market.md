<p><p>UV-resistant glass panels come in two main market types: transparent and opaque. Transparent UV-resistant glass panels are clear and allow light to pass through, making them ideal for applications where visibility is important such as windows, skylights, and greenhouse panels. Opaque UV-resistant glass panels are non-transparent and block out light, making them suitable for privacy applications such as walls, room dividers, and doors. Both types of UV-resistant glass panels provide protection against harmful UV rays while offering different aesthetic and functional benefits based on their transparency.</p></p>
