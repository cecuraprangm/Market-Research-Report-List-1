<p><ul><li>Reaction type</li><li>Additive type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic phosphorus flame retardants can be classified into two main types: reaction type and additive type. Reaction type flame retardants chemically react with the material they are applied to, forming a protective char layer that inhibits combustion. Additive type flame retardants are simply mixed into the material and function by physically interrupting the combustion process. Both types of flame retardants play a crucial role in improving the fire resistance of various materials, making them essential for ensuring fire safety in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
