<p><strong>The Commercial Floor Cleaning Maintenance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corrective Maintenance</li><li>Preventive Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrective maintenance refers to the type of commercial floor cleaning maintenance that is performed to fix or rectify issues or damages. It involves addressing problems that have already occurred, such as replacing damaged floor tiles or repairing malfunctioning equipment. On the other hand, preventive maintenance focuses on avoiding future problems and maintaining the condition of the floor. This includes regular cleaning, inspection, and maintenance tasks like polishing the floors, applying protective coatings, or servicing equipment to prevent breakdowns or deterioration. Both types of maintenance are aimed at keeping commercial floors in optimal condition and minimizing disruptions to business operations.</p></p>
