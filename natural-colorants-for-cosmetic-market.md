<p><p>Natural colorants for cosmetics can be categorized into three main types: plant colorants, animal colorants, and microbial colorants. Plant colorants are derived from various parts of plants such as roots, leaves, and flowers. Animal colorants are obtained from sources like insects, crustaceans, and marine organisms. Microbial colorants are produced by microorganisms like bacteria and yeast. These natural colorants are increasingly preferred by consumers due to their sustainability, eco-friendly nature, and safe usage in cosmetic products.</p></p>
