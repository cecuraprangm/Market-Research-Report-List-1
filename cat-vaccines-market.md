<p><p>Cat vaccines market consists of various types including attenuated live vaccines, conjugate vaccines, inactivated vaccines, subunit vaccines, toxoid vaccines, DNA vaccines, and recombinant vaccines. Attenuated live vaccines contain weakened forms of viruses, while conjugate vaccines combine antigens from different pathogens. Inactivated vaccines use killed pathogens, subunit vaccines contain specific antigens, toxoid vaccines use toxins that have been made harmless, DNA vaccines rely on genetic material, and recombinant vaccines use genetically engineered proteins. Each type offers unique benefits in protecting cats from diseases.</p></p>
