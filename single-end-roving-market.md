<p><ul><li>Continuous Single End Roving</li><li>Chopped Single End Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-end roving is a type of fiber reinforcement used in composite materials. Continuous Single End Roving is a long fiber strand wound onto a spool, providing strength and structural integrity. Chopped Single End Roving consists of short fiber strands cut to a specific length, ideal for applications requiring easy handling and uniform distribution. Both types cater to different market needs, with continuous roving being suitable for high-performance applications and chopped roving being more versatile for various industries.</p></p>
