<p><p>In the smartphone market, various types of technologies are employed for 3D imaging. Components like VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser), camera modules, narrow band filters, lenses, infrared receivers, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors, and other elements play crucial roles. VCSEL provides the laser source for depth sensing, camera modules capture the images, narrow band filters help in improving sensor performance, lenses focus the light, infrared receivers receive the emitted light, CMOS sensors convert light into electrical signals, and other components assist in enhancing overall imaging capabilities. Together, these technologies enable smartphones to capture and reproduce three-dimensional images.</p></p>
