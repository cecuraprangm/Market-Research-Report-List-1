<p><p>Data Line Common Mode Chokes are divided into three market types based on their speed capabilities: Super High Speed, High Speed, and General Speed. Super High Speed chokes are specifically designed to handle data transmission at ultra-fast speeds, such as in fiber optic networks. High Speed chokes are suitable for applications that require high data transfer rates, like in high-speed Ethernet connections. General Speed chokes are more versatile and can be used in a wide range of applications that don't require extremely high data speeds but still need reliable noise suppression and signal integrity.</p></p>
