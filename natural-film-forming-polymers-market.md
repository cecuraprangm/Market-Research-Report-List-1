<p><p>Natural Film Forming Polymers come in two main types: Water Soluble and Oil Soluble. Water soluble polymers form a film when dissolved in water, making them suitable for applications such as in adhesives, coatings, and packaging. On the other hand, oil soluble polymers form a film when dissolved in oil, making them ideal for applications such as in cosmetics, personal care products, and pharmaceuticals. Both types of polymers serve as effective film-forming agents in various industries.</p></p>
