<p><p>Performance plasma cutting machines are classified into two main market types: manual and automatic. </p><p>Manual plasma cutting machines require the operator to manually control the cutting process by adjusting settings and guiding the torch along the desired cutting path. These machines are typically used for smaller scale operations and offer more flexibility in terms of cutting patterns.</p><p>On the other hand, automatic plasma cutting machines are equipped with computerized systems that can automatically control the cutting process, allowing for higher precision and efficiency. These machines are commonly used in industrial settings for large scale production.</p></p>
