<p><p>Flex-rigid PCB market includes Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer options. Single Layer flex-rigid PCBs are simple, with all components on one side, ideal for basic applications. Double Layer flex-rigid PCBs have components on both sides, offering more design flexibility. Multi-Layer flex-rigid PCBs have multiple layers, providing additional space for components and more complex circuit designs. Each type caters to different needs, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option for their specific requirements.</p></p>
