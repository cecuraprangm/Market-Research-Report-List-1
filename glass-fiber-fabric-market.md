<p><p>The Glass-Fiber Fabric market is segmented into three types: Alkali-free Glass Fiber, Medium-alkali Glass Fiber, and High-alkali Glass Fiber. Alkali-free Glass Fiber is made without alkali metals and is used in applications requiring excellent chemical resistance and high strength. Medium-alkali Glass Fiber contains a moderate amount of alkali metals and is suitable for applications needing good corrosion resistance. High-alkali Glass Fiber has a high content of alkali metals and is commonly used in applications requiring superior alkali and acid resistance. These types provide different properties to meet various industry needs.</p></p>
