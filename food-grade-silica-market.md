<p><strong>The Food Grade Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Precipitated Silica</li><li>Fumed Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food Grade Silica market has two main types: Precipitated Silica and Fumed Silica. Precipitated Silica is produced by adding a mineral acid to a solution of sodium silicate to create a solid material, which is then washed and dried. It is widely used in food products as an anti-caking agent and thickener. Fumed Silica, on the other hand, is produced by burning silicon tetrachloride in a flame of hydrogen and oxygen. It is commonly used in food products as a thickening agent and stabilizer.</p></p>
