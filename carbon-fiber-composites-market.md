<p><p>Carbon fiber composites are divided into different types based on the material they are combined with. The main types are Carbon Fiber-Resin Composite, where carbon fiber is combined with resin for applications requiring lightweight and high strength materials. Carbon Fiber-Metal Composite combines carbon fiber with metals for increased strength and durability. Carbon Fiber-Ceramics Composite utilizes carbon fiber with ceramics for applications requiring high-temperature resistance. Other markets include combinations with different materials such as plastics, wood, or glass, offering a wide range of properties and applications for carbon fiber composites.</p></p>
