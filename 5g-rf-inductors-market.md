Film type inductors are made using a thin film or foil and are preferred for their high frequency performance. Multilayer type inductors are made using multiple layers of conductive material and are known for their compact size and high efficiency. These different types cater to diverse requirements in the rapidly evolving 5G technology landscape.</p></p>
