<p><p>The biodegradable freezer bags market is segmented into various types. Poly Lactic Acid (PLA) bags are made from renewable resources like corn starch and are compostable. Starch-based bags are derived from plant-based materials and are also compostable. Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA) bags are produced by bacterial fermentation of renewable resources and are fully biodegradable. Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) bags are made from petroleum-based materials and completely decompose under certain conditions. Cellulose-based bags are derived from wood pulp and are compostable. Other types include bags made from other natural materials.</p></p>
