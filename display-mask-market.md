<p><p>The display mask market includes two main types - photomasks and shadow masks. Photomasks are used in photolithography processes to transfer patterns onto semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing of electronic devices. Shadow masks, on the other hand, are used in cathode ray tube (CRT) display technology to control the electron beam and create images on the screen. Both types play a critical role in the production of various electronic displays, ensuring high resolution and accurate image reproduction.</p></p>
