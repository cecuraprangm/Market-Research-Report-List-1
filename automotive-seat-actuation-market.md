<p><p>The Automotive Seat Actuation Market is divided into two main types: Electric Seat Actuation System (ESAS) and Manual Seat Actuation System (MSAS). ESAS utilizes electric motors to adjust seat positions, providing more convenience and precision. On the other hand, MSAS requires manual operation by using levers or knobs to adjust the seat positions. ESAS is preferred in modern vehicles for its ease of use and advanced features, while MSAS is still found in some vehicles for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
