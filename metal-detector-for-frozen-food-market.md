<p><p>Metal detectors for frozen food can be categorized into three main types: Pulse Induction, Very Low Frequency, and Beat-frequency Oscillation. Pulse Induction detectors are ideal for detecting metals in conductive products like frozen foods. Very Low Frequency detectors are best suited for finding smaller metal contaminants in non-conductive foods. Beat-frequency Oscillation detectors are commonly used in the frozen food industry due to their ability to detect a wide range of metal contaminants. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the food processing operation.</p></p>
