<p><p>The Ready Mixed Concrete (RMX) market consists of three types: Transit Mixed Concrete, Shrink Mixed Concrete, and Central Mixed Concrete. Transit Mixed Concrete involves the mixing of concrete in transit, as the name suggests. Shrink Mixed Concrete is partially mixed at the plant and the remaining mixing is completed on site. Central Mixed Concrete is fully mixed at a plant and then transported to the construction site. These different market types offer various advantages and are chosen based on specific project requirements, location, and transportation logistics.</p></p>
