<p><ul><li>Extrusion Blow Molding</li><li>Injection Blow Molding</li><li>Stretch Blow Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Large blow molded products market consists of various types such as extrusion blow molding, injection blow molding, and stretch blow molding. Extrusion blow molding involves pushing molten plastic through a die to form a hollow tube, which is then shaped into the desired product. Injection blow molding combines injection molding and blow molding to create complex shapes with precise dimensions. Stretch blow molding is commonly used for producing bottles and containers by stretching a heated tube of polymer material and blowing it into a mold.</p></p>
