<p><ul><li>VVI Stimulators</li><li>V00 Stimulators</li><li>AAI Stimulators</li><li>A00 Stimulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temporary cardiac stimulators are devices used to regulate the heart rate in patients with temporary cardiac issues. There are four main types of temporary cardiac stimulators: VVI stimulators, which sense and pace the ventricles only; V00 stimulators, which do not sense the heart's activity and pace the ventricles continuously; AAI stimulators, which sense and pace the atria only; and A00 stimulators, which do not sense the heart's activity and pace the atria continuously. Each type is used based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
