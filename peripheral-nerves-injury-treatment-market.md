<p><p>Peripheral nerve injury treatment market includes various types of treatments such as direct repair, nerve grafting, nerve conduit, and other emerging treatments. Direct repair involves suturing the damaged nerve ends together, while nerve grafting involves using a section of a healthy nerve from another part of the body to bridge the gap in the damaged nerve. Nerve conduit utilizes synthetic tubes or biological materials to guide the regrowth of the damaged nerve. Other emerging treatments include nerve transfer, neurostimulation, and stem cell therapy, which aim to enhance nerve regeneration and functional recovery.</p></p>
