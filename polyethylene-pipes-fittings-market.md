<p><p>Polyethylene pipes & fittings are commonly categorized into four types based on the material used: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX or XLPE), and Others. LDPE pipes are flexible and commonly used in irrigation systems. HDPE pipes are known for their durability and are widely used in water and gas distribution. PEX or XLPE pipes have excellent chemical resistance and are used in plumbing applications. The Others category includes specialty polyethylene pipes for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
