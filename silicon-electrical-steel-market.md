<p><strong>The Silicon Electrical Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Type</li><li>Non-oriented Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon electrical steel is primarily divided into two types: oriented type and non-oriented type. Oriented type silicone electrical steel is designed for applications that require high efficiency and low losses, such as transformers and electric motors. Non-oriented type silicone electrical steel, on the other hand, is suitable for applications where the magnetization of the steel is not specifically directed, such as in rotating machinery and small motors. Both types play a crucial role in the electrical engineering industry, catering to different needs and specifications.</p></p>
