<p><p>Approved COVID-19 vaccines can be classified into two main types: component viral vaccines and whole virus vaccines. Component viral vaccines are developed by using a specific part or component of the virus, such as the spike protein, to trigger an immune response. On the other hand, whole virus vaccines utilize the complete, inactivated or weakened virus to stimulate the body's defense system. Both types aim to provide immunity against COVID-19 but employ different approaches in terms of viral components used in the vaccine development process.</p></p>
