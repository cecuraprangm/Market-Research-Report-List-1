<p><p>TypeC PD Control Chip Market is classified into two types - upstream-facing port (UFP) and downstream-facing port (DFP) markets. UFP refers to the source of power, such as a power adapter or charger, while DFP refers to the sink of power, like a smartphone or laptop. UFP market includes products that supply power, while DFP market encompasses devices that consume power. Understanding these distinctions is crucial for manufacturers and consumers to ensure compatibility and efficient power delivery in various electronic devices.</p></p>
