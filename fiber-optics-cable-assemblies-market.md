<p><p>Fiber optics cable assemblies are used for transmitting data through light signals. There are two main types of fiber optics cable assemblies: single mode and multiple mode. </p><p>Single mode fiber optics cable assemblies are designed for long-distance transmission with a single pathway for light to travel, resulting in higher transmission speeds and less signal loss. Multiple mode fiber optics cable assemblies, on the other hand, are used for shorter distances and have multiple pathways for light to travel, resulting in lower transmission speeds but more cost-effective solutions. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
