<p><p>Magnetic Microspheres can be categorized into three main types based on their coating and functionality. Coated Magnetic Microspheres have a protective layer around the magnetic core, providing stability and preventing aggregation. Coupled Magnetic Microspheres involve the attachment of molecules or ligands to the surface of the microspheres, allowing for specific interactions in various applications. Conjugated Magnetic Microspheres have biomolecules or antibodies covalently bound to the surface for targeted detection or drug delivery. Each type serves specific purposes in research, diagnostics, and therapeutics.</p></p>
