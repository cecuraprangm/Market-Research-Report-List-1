<p><p>Polyethylene embossed films are categorized based on their thickness into four different market types: below 20 μm, 21-100 μm, 101-200 μm, and above 200 μm. Films below 20 μm are ultra-thin and commonly used for packaging applications where flexibility is crucial. Films between 21-100 μm are versatile and widely used in various industries. Films in the 101-200 μm range offer enhanced durability and protection. Films above 200 μm are thick and heavy-duty, suitable for demanding applications requiring robust packaging solutions.</p></p>
