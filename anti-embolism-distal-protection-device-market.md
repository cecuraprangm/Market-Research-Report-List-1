<p><p>The anti-embolism distal protection device market is categorized into single layer filter and double layer filter types. Single layer filters consist of a single mesh layer to capture embolic material, while double layer filters have an additional outer layer for enhanced protection against emboli. These devices are used during procedures such as angioplasty and stenting to prevent embolism by capturing debris that may break loose during the procedure. The choice between single and double layer filters depends on the specific needs of the patient and the complexity of the procedure.</p></p>
