Microbial biorational fungicides contain microorganisms that suppress or inhibit the growth of fungi. Non-organic biorational fungicides are synthetic compounds that have minimal impacts on the environment and human health. All these types of biorational fungicides aim to provide effective control of fungal diseases while minimizing adverse effects on the environment and human health.</p></p>
