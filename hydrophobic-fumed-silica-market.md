<p><p>Hydrophobic fumed silica is classified into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I is generally used in applications that require high hydrophobicity, such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants. This type offers superior dispersion and reinforcement properties. On the other hand, Type II is preferred for applications where high thixotropy and thickening characteristics are necessary, such as in printing inks and silicone elastomers. Both types play a crucial role in various industries due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
