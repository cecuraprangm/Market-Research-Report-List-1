<p><p>Surgical wound staplers are medical devices used to close surgical incisions or wounds. They are categorized into two types: disposable and non-disposable. Disposable staplers are designed for single-use and are discarded after each surgery. They reduce the risk of cross-contamination and infection. On the other hand, non-disposable staplers are designed for multiple uses and require cleaning and sterilization between surgeries. Both types of staplers play a vital role in wound closure during surgical procedures but differ in terms of their intended use and disposal methods.</p></p>
