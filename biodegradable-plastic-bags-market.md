<p><p>Biodegradable plastic bags market can be segmented into three types: Polylactic Acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA is derived from renewable resources such as corn starch or sugarcane, making it compostable. PHA is produced by bacteria and is biodegradable in various environments. Starch blends are a combination of starch and biodegradable polymers, offering a cost-effective and environmentally friendly option. These types of biodegradable plastic bags are gaining popularity due to increasing environmental concerns and regulations on single-use plastics.</p></p>
