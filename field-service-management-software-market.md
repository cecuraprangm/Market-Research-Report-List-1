<p><p>Field Service Management (FSM) software offers solutions for managing and optimizing field operations. The market can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. Cloud-based FSM software, on the other hand, is accessed and managed over the internet, eliminating the need for local installations and allowing for remote access, automatic updates, and scalability.
