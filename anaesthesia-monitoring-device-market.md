<p><p>Anaesthesia monitoring devices come in three main types - basic anaesthesia monitors, integrated anaesthesia workstations, and advanced anaesthesia monitors. Basic anaesthesia monitors provide essential vital sign monitoring during procedures. Integrated anaesthesia workstations combine monitoring with drug delivery systems for more streamlined care. Advanced anaesthesia monitors offer advanced monitoring capabilities such as depth of anaesthesia monitoring and enhanced connectivity features. Each type of device plays a crucial role in ensuring patient safety and optimal anaesthesia delivery during medical procedures.</p></p>
