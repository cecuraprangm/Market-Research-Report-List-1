<p><p>Head-Mounted Display (HMD) market can be categorized into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired HMDs are connected to external devices like computers or gaming consoles through physical cables, providing a stable and high-quality connection. On the other hand, wireless HMDs use technologies such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to connect wirelessly to devices, offering more freedom of movement and flexibility. Both types cater to different user preferences and requirements, with wired HMDs being preferred for professional and high-performance applications, while wireless HMDs are more suitable for consumer and entertainment use.</p></p>
