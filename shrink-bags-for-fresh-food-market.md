<p><p>Shrink bags for fresh food market can be classified into three main types based on their barrier properties: EVOH based, PVDC based, and others. EVOH based shrink bags are known for their excellent oxygen barrier properties, making them ideal for extending the shelf life of fresh foods. PVDC based shrink bags provide superior barrier protection against moisture and gases. Other types of shrink bags may offer a combination of barrier properties depending on the specific requirements of the food product being packaged.</p></p>
