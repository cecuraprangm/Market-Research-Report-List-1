<p><p>The online airline reservation system market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. In the cloud-based market, the reservation system is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This allows airlines to have flexible and scalable solutions without the need for extensive infrastructure investment. On the other hand, the on-premises market refers to reservation systems that are physically located within an airline's own premises. This gives airlines more control over their data and security, but requires higher upfront costs and maintenance.</p></p>
