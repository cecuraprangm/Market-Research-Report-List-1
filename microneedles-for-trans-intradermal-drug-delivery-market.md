<p><p>The market for microneedles used in transdermal and intradermal drug delivery can be classified into three main types: solid microneedles, hollow microneedles, and dissolvable microneedles. Solid microneedles are made of solid materials and create tiny punctures in the skin to deliver drugs. Hollow microneedles, on the other hand, have small channels through which drugs can be directly injected into the skin. Dissolvable microneedles are made of biodegradable materials that dissolve upon insertion, releasing the drug into the skin. These different types cater to various drug delivery requirements.</p></p>
