<p><p>The Shear Wave Transducer market includes various types of sensors, such as Normal Incidence Sensor and Radial Incidence Sensor. A Normal Incidence Sensor is designed to send and receive shear wave signals in a direction perpendicular to the surface of the material being tested. On the other hand, a Radial Incidence Sensor is used to send and receive shear waves in a direction that is parallel to the surface of the material being tested. These different market types cater to specific testing requirements and applications.</p></p>
