<p><p>A metal shell and tube heat exchanger is a type of heat exchanger that is widely used in various industries for the transfer of heat between two fluids. It consists of a shell with multiple tubes inside it, through which the hot and cold fluids flow in separate paths. The hot fluid transfers heat to the cold fluid through the tube walls, resulting in the desired temperature exchange.</p><p>The metal shell and tube heat exchanger market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the primary factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for efficient heat transfer solutions in industries such as power generation, oil and gas, chemical, and food and beverage.
