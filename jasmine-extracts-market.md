<p><p>Jasmine extracts can be classified into two market types: organic extract and conventional extract. Organic extracts are derived from jasmine flowers that have been grown without the use of synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or genetic modification. These extracts are produced using natural and sustainable farming methods, appealing to consumers who prioritize sustainability and chemical-free products. On the other hand, conventional extracts are made from jasmine flowers that may have been cultivated using conventional agricultural practices incorporating synthetic chemicals. These extracts are more widely available and cater to consumers who may have different preferences or prioritize affordability over organic certifications.</p></p>
