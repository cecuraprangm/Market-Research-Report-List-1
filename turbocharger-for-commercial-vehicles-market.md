<p><p>Turbochargers for commercial vehicles come in two main types: mechanical turbocharging and exhaust turbocharging. Mechanical turbocharging uses mechanical means to compress air and increase engine power, while exhaust turbocharging utilizes the energy in the vehicle's exhaust gas to drive a turbine that compresses air. Both types of turbocharging are commonly used in commercial vehicles to improve fuel efficiency and power output. They are essential components in modern commercial vehicle engines, helping them perform efficiently and effectively on the road.</p></p>
