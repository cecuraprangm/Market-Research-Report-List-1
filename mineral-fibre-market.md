<p><ul><li>Glasswool</li><li>Rockwool</li><li>Slagwool</li><li>Glass Filaments</li><li>Ceramic Fibres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral fibre market includes various types such as Glasswool, Rockwool, Slagwool, Glass Filaments, and Ceramic Fibres. Glasswool is made from recycled glass bottles and is widely used for thermal and acoustic insulation. Rockwool is made from volcanic rock and is known for its fire resistance. Slagwool is made from blast furnace slag and is used in high-temperature applications. Glass Filaments are thin strands of glass used in reinforcements. Ceramic Fibres are made from alumina or silica and are heat resistant.</p></p>
