<p><p>Ultrafine Zinc Oxide market types include Direct Process (America Method), Indirect Process (French Method), and Wet Chemical Process. Direct Process involves the reduction of zinc ore with coal in a furnace, while Indirect Process uses zinc vapors to form zinc oxide. Wet Chemical Process involves dissolving zinc in sulfuric acid and then precipitating as zinc hydroxide, followed by calcination to obtain zinc oxide. Each method has its own advantages and characteristics, catering to different industrial applications and requirements in the market.</p></p>
