<p><p>The Automotive ABS Sensor market consists of two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a Hall effect sensor to detect the rotational speed of the wheel and are commonly used in modern vehicles for their accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors use a magnetic field to measure the wheel speed and are often found in older vehicle models. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of the Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) in vehicles.</p></p>
