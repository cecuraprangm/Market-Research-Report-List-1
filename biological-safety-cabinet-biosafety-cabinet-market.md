Class II Type B cabinets provide protection for working with hazardous biological agents by directing exhaust air to the outside through a dedicated duct. Class III Type cabinets provide the highest level of containment, with all operations performed through glove ports and a completely sealed work area to prevent any exposure to hazardous materials.</p></p>
