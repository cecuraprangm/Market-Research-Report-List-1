Flat plate collectors are the most common type, consisting of a dark absorber plate under a transparent cover. Evacuated tube collectors use a series of parallel glass tubes to capture sunlight and convert it to heat. Other types may include concentrating collectors or hybrid systems. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in the solar energy industry.</p></p>
