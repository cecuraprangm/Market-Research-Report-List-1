<p><strong>The Microfluidic Droplet Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microfluidic droplet chips are categorized into two types based on their operation: passive and active. Passive microfluidic droplet chips rely on external forces such as gravity or capillary forces to manipulate droplets within the chip. In contrast, active microfluidic droplet chips utilize on-chip actuators such as valves, pumps, or electrodes to precisely control droplet movement. Both passive and active microfluidic droplet chips have unique advantages and applications, with passive chips being more simple and cost-effective, while active chips offer greater flexibility and control.</p></p>
