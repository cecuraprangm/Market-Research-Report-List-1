Vincristine blocks cancer cell growth by disrupting their ability to divide. Cyclophosphamide is an immunosuppressive drug that prevents cancer cells from dividing. Etoposide inhibits the growth of cancer cells by interfering with their DNA. Irinotecan is a chemotherapy drug that interferes with cancer cell growth by inhibiting an enzyme called topoisomerase.
