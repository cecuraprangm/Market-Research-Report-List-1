<p><strong>The Drying Systems for Softgels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumbler Dryer & Drying Tunnels</li><li>In-Line Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the drying systems for softgels market, there are two main types: tumbler dryers and drying tunnels. Tumbler dryers are enclosed machines that use rotation to gently dry softgel capsules. They are compact and suitable for small-scale production. On the other hand, drying tunnels are larger systems that employ a conveyor belt to transport the softgels through a heated chamber, ensuring uniform drying. These tunnels are commonly used in high-volume production. Both tumbler dryers and drying tunnels are efficient methods for drying softgel capsules and play a crucial role in the in-line drying market.</p></p>
