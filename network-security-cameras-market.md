<p><strong>The Network Security Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Cameras</li><li>DeCentralized IP Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized IP Cameras are connected to a centralized network video recorder (NVR) which stores and manages the recorded footage. These cameras feature high image quality and advanced analytics capabilities but have a single point of failure. On the other hand, DeCentralized IP Cameras have built-in storage capabilities, allowing them to function independently without the need for a central NVR. While they may lack some advanced features, they offer greater flexibility and scalability in terms of installation and storage options.</p></p>
