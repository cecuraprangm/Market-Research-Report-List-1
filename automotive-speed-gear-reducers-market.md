<p><p>Automotive speed gear reducers refer to the different types of transmission systems used in vehicles to control and optimize speed and power delivery. The four main types are AT (automatic transmission), CVT (continuously variable transmission), DCT (dual-clutch transmission), and others (including manual transmissions and semi-automatic transmissions). AT systems use a fluid coupling or torque converter to change gears automatically, while CVT systems offer a smooth and seamless transition between gears. DCT systems use two separate clutches for faster and more efficient gear changes.</p></p>
