<p><p>Pigment Additive Film Market consists of two main types: inorganic and organic. Inorganic pigment additives are derived from metallic compounds and provide durable and stable characteristics to films. They are often used in applications requiring high heat resistance and lightfastness. On the other hand, organic pigment additives are made from carbon-based compounds and offer a wide range of color choices and vibrant hues. They are commonly used in applications where color intensity and versatility are important factors. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the properties of films in various industries.</p></p>
