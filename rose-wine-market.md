<p><p>Rose wine can be produced using different methods, each impacting the flavor and color of the final product. The maceration method involves allowing the grape skins to be in contact with the juice for a short period of time, giving the wine its pink hue. The Saignée or “bled” method involves bleeding off some of the juice from red wine fermentation to create a rose wine. The blending method involves mixing red and white wines together to create a pink wine. Each method creates unique characteristics in the rose wine market.</p></p>
