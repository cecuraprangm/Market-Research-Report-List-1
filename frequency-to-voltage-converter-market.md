<p><strong>The Frequency-to-voltage Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Channel</li><li>Differential Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency-to-voltage converters are used to convert frequency signals into corresponding voltage outputs. In analog channel market, converters are designed to process single-ended signals and provide a continuous, smooth output voltage proportional to the input frequency. On the other hand, in the differential channel market, converters are capable of processing differential signals with higher accuracy and noise rejection. They provide differential outputs based on the input frequency difference between two signals. Both types cater to different application requirements and offer various advantages in signal processing.</p></p>
