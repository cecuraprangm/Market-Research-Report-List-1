<p><p>Cleanroom wipes are commonly divided into two main types: woven and non-woven. Woven wipes are made of tightly bound fibers that provide added strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty cleaning tasks. On the other hand, non-woven wipes are made of synthetic fibers that offer excellent absorbency and softness, making them suitable for delicate surfaces. Both types of wipes are widely used in cleanroom environments to maintain cleanliness and prevent contamination during critical processes.</p></p>
