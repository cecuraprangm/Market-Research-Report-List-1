<p><p>Methyl Methacrylate Monomer market is divided into three major types based on the production methods: ACH Method, Isobutylene Method, and Ethylene Method. The ACH Method involves the oxidation of acetone and hydrogen cyanide. The Isobutylene Method involves the use of isobutylene and formaldehyde as raw materials. The Ethylene Method involves the production of MMA from ethylene and carbon monoxide. Each of these methods has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
