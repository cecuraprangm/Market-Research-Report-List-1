<p><strong>The Gooseneck Trailer Hitches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Under-bed Gooseneck</li><li>Over/Above-bed Gooseneck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gooseneck trailer hitches come in two main market types - under-bed gooseneck and over/above-bed gooseneck. Under-bed gooseneck hitches are installed beneath the truck bed, allowing for a completely flat surface when not in use, which is ideal for maximizing cargo space. Over/above-bed gooseneck hitches are mounted above the truck bed, providing easier access for installing and removing the hitch. Both types offer reliable towing capabilities and are popular choices for hauling heavy loads with stability and security.</p></p>
