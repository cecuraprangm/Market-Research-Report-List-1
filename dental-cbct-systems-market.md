<p><ul><li>Small Field</li><li>Medium Field</li><li>Large Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental CBCT systems refer to the Cone Beam Computed Tomography technology used in dentistry. There are different types of dental CBCT systems categorized based on field size, namely Small Field, Medium Field, and Large Field. Small Field systems capture images of a specific area, such as a single tooth or a section of the jaw. Medium Field systems scan a larger area, often the upper and lower jaw. Large Field systems have the widest range and can capture images of the entire skull and supporting structures, providing a more comprehensive examination.</p></p>
