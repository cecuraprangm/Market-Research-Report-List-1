<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li><li>Universal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-axis CNC milling machine market consists of three main types - horizontal, vertical, and universal. </p><p>Horizontal machines have the spindle mounted horizontally, allowing for easy chip removal and better stability for heavy cutting. Vertical machines have the spindle mounted vertically, making them more suitable for intricate and detailed workpieces. </p><p>Universal machines combine the features of both horizontal and vertical machines, offering versatility and flexibility in machining various types of materials. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
