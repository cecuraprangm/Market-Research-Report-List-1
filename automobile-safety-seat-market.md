<p><p>The Automobile Safety Seat market is divided into four main types: Infant Seat, Booster Seat, Combination Seat, and Convertible Seat. Infant Seats are designed for newborns and young babies, providing rear-facing protection. Booster Seats are meant for older children who have outgrown their infant seats and need a boost to use the car's seat belt properly. Combination Seats can be used as both a forward-facing harness seat and a booster seat. Convertible Seats can be used in multiple positions, usually starting as a rear-facing seat and converting to forward-facing as the child grows.</p></p>
