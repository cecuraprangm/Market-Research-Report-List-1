<p><p>A spiral bevel gearbox is a type of gearbox that uses spiral bevel gears to transmit power between shafts that are at an angle to each other. Spiral bevel gearboxes are commonly used in various industrial applications such as mining, construction, and automotive industries.</p><p>The Spiral Bevel Gearbox Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
