Container/harness systems hold the main and reserve parachutes and secure them to the skydiver's body. Helmets offer head protection, while altimeters measure altitude. Automatic activation devices deploy the reserve parachute if the skydiver is in an emergency situation. Other accessories include goggles, gloves, and audible altimeters.</p></p>
