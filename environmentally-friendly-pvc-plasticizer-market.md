<p><p>Environmentally-friendly PVC plasticizers are classified into different types based on their chemical composition and properties. Adipate esters are versatile plasticizers with good low-temperature flexibility. Trimellitate esters offer high-temperature resistance and are suitable for applications where heat stability is critical. Epoxies esters provide excellent weathering and UV resistance. Benzoates are known for their high efficiency and low volatility. These different types of plasticizers cater to specific needs in the market while also being environmentally-friendly alternatives to traditional phthalate plasticizers.</p></p>
