<p><strong>The Acrylic Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Panel</li><li>Cast Acrylic Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic panel market can be segmented into two main types: extruded acrylic panels and cast acrylic panels. Extruded acrylic panels are manufactured using a process where acrylic pellets are melted and forced through a die, resulting in a more affordable and lightweight option. On the other hand, cast acrylic panels are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and allowing it to harden, producing a higher quality and more durable product. Both types have their own unique characteristics and are used for various applications in the market.</p></p>
