<p><p>Card dispensers can be categorized into two main types: ID card dispensers and RFID card dispensers. ID card dispensers are designed to securely dispense identification cards to authorized individuals, such as employees or students. These dispensers are often used in office buildings, schools, and other secure facilities. RFID card dispensers, on the other hand, are equipped with technology that allows them to dispense RFID cards, which contain embedded chips for tracking and identification purposes. These dispensers are commonly used in access control systems and transportation systems.</p></p>
