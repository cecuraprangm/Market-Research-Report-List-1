<p><p>GI stool testing is a diagnostic procedure that helps detect various gastrointestinal disorders. There are different types of GI stool tests available. Microscopy tests involve examining the stool under a microscope to identify pathogens. Ova & parasites tests detect the presence of eggs or parasites in the stool. Occult blood tests check for hidden blood in the stool, which can indicate various health conditions. Stool biomarkers tests measure specific substances in the stool to identify certain diseases. Other types of GI stool tests include tests for inflammation markers, bacterial infections, and digestive enzyme deficiencies.</p></p>
