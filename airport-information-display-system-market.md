<p><strong>The Airport Information Display System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Terminal Side Airport Information Display System</li><li>Air Side Airport Information Display System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport Information Display System (A-IDS) market can be categorized into two main types: Terminal Side A-IDS and Air Side A-IDS. Terminal Side A-IDS are installed inside the airport terminals to provide passengers with information such as flight schedules, gate assignments, and baggage carousel details. On the other hand, Air Side A-IDS are located outside on the airfield to display important information for pilots and ground crew, including runway assignments, weather updates, and aircraft parking instructions. Both types of A-IDS play a crucial role in ensuring efficient operations and seamless passenger experience at airports.</p></p>
