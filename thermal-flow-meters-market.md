<p><p>Thermal flow meters are used in various industries for measuring the flow of liquids and gases. The market for thermal flow meters includes different types such as ultrasonic, Coriolis, turbine, vortex, magnetic, differential pressure, and positive displacement meters. Ultrasonic meters use sound waves to measure flow, Coriolis meters measure mass flow based on the Coriolis effect, turbine meters use a rotor to measure flow velocity, vortex meters measure flow based on the vortex shedding principle, magnetic meters use electromagnetic signals, differential pressure meters measure flow based on pressure difference, and positive displacement meters measure flow by trapping a fixed volume of fluid.</p></p>
