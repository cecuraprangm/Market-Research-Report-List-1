<p><p>Continuous feed paper market consists of two main types: single layer and multi-layer paper. Single layer paper is commonly used for printing receipts, invoices, and shipping labels due to its simple structure and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, multi-layer paper is utilized for applications requiring multiple copies of the same document such as invoices or purchase orders. Multi-layer paper is popular in industries like retail, logistics, and finance for its ability to create duplicate and triplicate copies simultaneously.</p></p>
