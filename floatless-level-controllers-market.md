<p><p>Floatless level controllers are devices used to measure and control the level of liquids in tanks or vessels without the use of traditional float mechanisms. These controllers utilize various technologies such as ultrasonic sensors, conductivity probes, or pressure sensors to accurately measure liquid levels and provide precise control functions. They offer several advantages over float-based controllers, including better reliability, accuracy, and the ability to operate in a wider range of fluid types and conditions.</p><p>The floatless level controllers market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
