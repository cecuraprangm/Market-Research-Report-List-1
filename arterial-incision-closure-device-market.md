<p><p>Arterial incision closure device market consists of two main types: Passive Closure Devices and Active Closure Devices. Passive Closure Devices are designed to facilitate natural clot formation at the incision site, promoting prompt healing without the need for manual intervention. On the other hand, Active Closure Devices involve the use of mechanical or adhesive components to close arterial incisions. These devices offer more control over the closure process, reducing the risk of complications and ensuring secure closure of the incision site.</p></p>
