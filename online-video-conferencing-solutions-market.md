<p><p>Online video conferencing solutions market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions use servers and infrastructure provided by a third-party service provider, allowing users to access the video conferencing platform through the internet. On the other hand, on-premise solutions are installed and managed within the organization's own infrastructure, providing more control and security but requiring higher upfront investments and maintenance.</p></p>
