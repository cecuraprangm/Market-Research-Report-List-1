<p><p>Bone growth stimulants are used to promote bone healing and growth in orthopedic and dental surgeries. External bone growth stimulants are applied externally to the skin or near the surgical site to stimulate bone formation. Implanted bone growth stimulants are placed directly into the surgical site to enhance bone growth. Ultrasound bone growth stimulants use low-intensity pulsed ultrasound to promote bone healing. Bone morphogenetic protein and platelet-rich plasma are growth factors that can be used to stimulate bone growth. Other bone growth stimulant markets include electromagnetic stimulators and growth hormone therapies.</p></p>
