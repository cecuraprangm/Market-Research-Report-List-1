<p><p>Acousto-optic modulators (AOMs) are devices that control the intensity, frequency, and phase of a laser beam. There are two main types of AOMs: fiber-coupled AOMs and free-space AOMs. Fiber-coupled AOMs are designed to work with fiber optics systems, allowing for easy integration into existing setups. Free-space AOMs, on the other hand, are standalone devices that are used in open optical systems. Both types of AOMs offer precise control over laser beams, making them essential components in various applications such as spectroscopy, telecommunications, and laser processing.</p></p>
