<p><p>Non-selective herbicides are broad-spectrum weed killers that are effective against a wide range of plant species. Some common types of non-selective herbicides include paraquat, glyphosate, diquat, and other lesser-known herbicides. Paraquat is a fast-acting herbicide that kills plants upon contact, while glyphosate is a systemic herbicide that is absorbed by the plant and kills it from within. Diquat is a contact herbicide that disrupts photosynthesis in plants. Other non-selective herbicides may have varying modes of action and levels of effectiveness against different types of weeds.</p></p>
