<p><strong>The Ketone Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ketone resins are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their characteristics and applications. Type I ketone resins are typically used in printing inks, coatings, and adhesives due to their excellent color stability and high chemical resistance. On the other hand, Type II ketone resins are known for their high solubility and compatibility with various polymers, making them suitable for applications such as lamination, packaging, and composite materials. Both types of ketone resins play a vital role in various industries, offering unique properties and functionalities.</p></p>
