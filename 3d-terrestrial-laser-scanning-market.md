<p><p>3D Terrestrial Laser Scanning is a technology that uses lasers to capture highly detailed and accurate three-dimensional data of objects and environments. It involves the use of a stationary or handheld laser scanner that emits a laser beam and measures the time it takes for the beam to bounce back after hitting the target. This data is then processed to create a digital representation of the scanned area, commonly known as a point cloud.</p><p>The 3D Terrestrial Laser Scanning Market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period.
