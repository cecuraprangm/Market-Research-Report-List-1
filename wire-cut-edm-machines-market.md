<p><strong>The Wire-cut EDM Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Cut Flushing Type EDM</li><li>Wire-Cut Submerge Type EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire-cut EDM Machines market consists of two main types - Wire-Cut Flushing Type EDM and Wire-Cut Submerge Type EDM. The Wire-Cut Flushing Type EDM uses a continuous flow of dielectric fluid to flush away debris during the cutting process, while the Wire-Cut Submerge Type EDM submerges the workpiece in dielectric fluid, allowing for faster cutting speeds and improved accuracy. Both types offer high precision cutting capabilities and are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
