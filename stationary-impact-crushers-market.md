<p><p>Stationary impact crushers are machines used to crush materials in various industries. There are two main types: Horizontal Shaft Impact (HSI) crushers and Vertical Shaft Impact (VSI) crushers. </p><p>HSI crushers use a horizontal shaft to crush materials, offering a high reduction ratio and versatile applications. On the other hand, VSI crushers use a vertical shaft and are ideal for producing cubical-shaped end products. Both types are stationary, providing efficient crushing solutions for different material types and sizes.</p></p>
