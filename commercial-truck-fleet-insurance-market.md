<p><ul><li>Compulsory Insurance</li><li>Optional Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial truck fleet insurance market consists of two types of insurance coverage: compulsory insurance and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is a regulatory requirement that every commercial truck fleet must obtain to operate legally. It provides coverage for third-party liabilities, such as bodily injury or property damage caused by the fleet. On the other hand, optional insurance allows fleet owners to obtain additional coverage for their own assets, including the trucks themselves and any cargo they may be carrying. This optional coverage offers protection against theft, accidents, and damage to the fleet.</p></p>
