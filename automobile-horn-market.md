<p><p>The automobile horn market consists of two main types: Air Horns and Electric Horns. Air horns use compressed air to produce a loud sound, typically found in larger vehicles such as trucks and buses. Electric horns, on the other hand, use an electromagnetic system to generate sound and are commonly used in smaller vehicles like cars and motorcycles. Both types of horns serve the same purpose of alerting drivers and pedestrians of a vehicle's presence, but differ in their mechanism of producing sound.</p></p>
