<p><ul><li>Twisted Pair(Copper)</li><li>Coax(Copper)</li><li>Optic Cables(Fibre)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Signal data cables are classified into three main types: Twisted Pair (Copper), Coax (Copper), and Optic Cables (Fibre). Twisted Pair cables are made of two copper wires twisted together to reduce electrical interference. Coaxial cables consist of a copper core surrounded by insulation and a braided metal shield to protect against signal interference. Optic cables use pulses of light to transmit data through glass or plastic fibers. Each type has unique characteristics that make them suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
