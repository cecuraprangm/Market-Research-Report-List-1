<p><p>The 5G Base Transceiver Stations (BTS) market is categorized into four types: Macro, Small, Pico, and Femto. Macro BTS are large towers that provide coverage over a wide area. Small BTS are smaller stations designed to enhance coverage and capacity in high-traffic areas. Pico BTS are even smaller and are used for localized coverage in buildings or outdoor spaces. Femto BTS are tiny stations meant for providing coverage in small indoor areas like homes or offices. Each type serves a specific market need for 5G connectivity.</p></p>
