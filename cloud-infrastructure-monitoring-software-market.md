<p><ul><li>Public Cloud</li><li>Private Cloud</li><li>Hybrid Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud infrastructure monitoring software market consists of three types: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud. </p><p>Public cloud refers to the infrastructure that is owned and operated by a third-party cloud provider and made available to multiple organizations over the internet. It offers cost-efficiency and scalability.</p><p>Private cloud, on the other hand, is dedicated to a single organization and is managed internally or by a third-party service provider. It ensures greater control, security, and customization options.</p><p>Hybrid cloud combines elements of both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to store critical data and applications on a private cloud while leveraging the public cloud for less sensitive operations. This offers flexibility and cost optimization.</p></p>
