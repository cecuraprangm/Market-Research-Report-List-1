<p><p>Cold seal plastic films are categorized into different types based on their material composition. Polyolefin-based cold seal plastic films are made from ethylene or propylene polymers and are commonly used for packaging food products. Polypropylene cold seal plastic films provide high clarity and are ideal for retail packaging. Polyvinylchloride cold seal plastic films offer excellent barrier properties and are used for medical or pharmaceutical applications. Ethylene-butylene cold seal plastic films have good adhesion properties and are suitable for packaging perishable goods that require a longer shelf life.</p></p>
