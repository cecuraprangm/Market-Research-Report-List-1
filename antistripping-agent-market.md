<p><p>Antistripping agents are used in asphalt paving to prevent the loss of adhesion between asphalt binder and aggregate, which can lead to pavement failure. There are three main types of antistripping agents in the market: amine antistripping agents, lime antistripping agents, and other types such as polymers or acids. Amine antistripping agents work by forming a chemical bond with the asphalt binder, while lime antistripping agents work by increasing the overall adhesion between the binder and aggregate. Other types of antistripping agents may work through various mechanisms such as physical adhesion or chemical reactions.</p></p>
