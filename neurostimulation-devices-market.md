Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) involves implanting electrodes in the brain to manage conditions like Parkinson's disease. Sacral Nerve Stimulation (SNS) is used for bladder control and fecal incontinence. Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) involves stimulating the vagus nerve to treat epilepsy and depression.
