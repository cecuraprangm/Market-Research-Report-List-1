<p><strong>The Glass FIber Nonwoven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass Fiber Nonwoven Market consists of two main types: Wet-Laid Process and Dry-Laid Process. In Wet-Laid Process, glass fibers are formed into a nonwoven fabric by mixing them with a binder in a water-based solution and then laying them out on a conveyor belt. Dry-Laid Process involves directly laying out the glass fibers onto a moving belt without the use of water or any binders. Both processes result in high-quality and versatile nonwoven materials used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and filtration.</p></p>
