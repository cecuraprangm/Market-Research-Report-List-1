<p><strong>The Specialty Yeast Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Yeast Extract</li><li>Autolytic Yeast</li><li>Other Yeast Derivatives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty yeast market types include yeast extract, autolytic yeast, and other yeast derivatives. Yeast extract is a flavoring agent derived from yeast cells through processes such as autolysis. Autolytic yeast is produced by allowing yeast cells to self-digest their own proteins and release flavor compounds. Other yeast derivatives are byproducts obtained from yeast fermentation processes, such as beta-glucans and yeast cell walls. These specialty yeast products are used in food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and animal feed industries for their unique flavors, textures, and nutritional benefits.</p></p>
