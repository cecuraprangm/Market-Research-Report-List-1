<p><p>Trimethylolpropane (TMP) Flakes market can be classified into two types: Sodium Formate Method and Aldehyde Hydrogenation Method. The Sodium Formate Method involves reacting formaldehyde and sodium hydroxide with trimethylolpropane to produce TMP flakes. On the other hand, the Aldehyde Hydrogenation Method involves catalytic hydrogenation of formaldehyde and isobutyraldehyde with hydrogen gas to produce TMP flakes. Both methods result in the production of high-quality TMP flakes used in various industries such as resin and coating production.</p></p>
