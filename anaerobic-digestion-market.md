<p><p>Anaerobic digestion is a process where organic materials are broken down by bacteria in the absence of oxygen. The byproducts of this process include biogas and digestate. Biogas is a renewable energy source consisting mainly of methane and carbon dioxide, which can be used for heat and power generation. Digestate is the nutrient-rich residue left after the anaerobic digestion process, which can be used as a fertilizer in agriculture.
